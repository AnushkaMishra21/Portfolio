2048

"2048" is a popular and addictive website puzzle game where players slide numbered tiles on a grid. When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one, doubling the number. The goal is to reach the tile with the number 2048 by strategically combining tiles while preventing the grid from filling up. The game tests your logic and planning skills, offering a challenging and rewarding experience.
